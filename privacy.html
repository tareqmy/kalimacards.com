Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 14, 2026

At KalimaCards, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website, application, and Google authentication services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal personal data to provide and improve your learning experience:

  • Account Information: If you sign in via Google OAuth, we receive your public Google Profile information, including your email address, name, and profile picture URL.
  • Application Data: We store your learning statistics, exam scores, marked known/learning words, starred words, and configuration preferences to synchronize your progress across devices.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To verify your identity and manage your user account.
  • To synchronize your vocabulary learning progress and statistics between your devices.
  • To personalize your study session interface with your custom preferences.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your data privacy. We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with any third-party marketing services or external platforms. Your information is only used within KalimaCards to provide your personal synchronization database.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your user profile and study data. Authentication and data transfers are encrypted using Secure Socket Layer (SSL/TLS) protocols.

5. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. At any time, you can:

  • Sign out of the application to stop data synchronization.
  • Delete your account and all associated synchronized study statistics directly through the user profile settings, or by contacting us.
