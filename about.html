Tareq Mohammad Yousuf

Software Architect & Team Lead

Tareq Mohammad Yousuf is a veteran Software Architect, Developer, and Team Lead with over 20 years of experience building high-scale, reliable enterprise systems. As a specialist in Java, cloud infrastructure, and DevOps, he designs robust backend architectures and automated pipelines that power modern digital solutions.

With a passion for engineering excellence, Tareq is an active contributor to open-source systems, notably in telephony integrations (Asterisk-Java, ari4java), and is the creator of KalimaCards—an active recall tool designed to simplify Quranic vocabulary study.
